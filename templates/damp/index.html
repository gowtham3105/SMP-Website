Welcome!

The Department Academic Mentorship Program (DAMP) team at IIT Dharwad consists of exuberant and highly motivated students working towards providing the necessary help and guidance to all sophomores.

For Sophomore

Damp will help you to cope up with your transition into your department.

Resolve Issues

Damp will help you in academic as well as non academic issuses.

Acadamic Suggestions

Damp will try its best to help students with backlogs.

Introduction

The Department Academic Mentorship Program (DAMP) aims to create a support system for students facing academic as well as personal difficulties. The DAMP also provides senior mentors to the sophomores entering the CSE, EE and ME depatment. The DAMP Team aims to provide easy access to information regarding courses offered by the departments, research projects being undertaken, internships and the like. We strive to maintain the curriculum’s relevance to the field in co-ordination with faculty as well as the Department Council. Feel free to contact our team for any kind of difficulties.
